J Jewelry Co. is a small family owned jewelry store in the heart of the Central Coast. We are located in Downtown San Luis Obispo, CA. The Andersen family has been running J Jewelry Co. for the last ten years. We have grown our team over the years and currently have fifteen employees. J Jewelry Co. does in-store shopping only so we work directly with our customers.
