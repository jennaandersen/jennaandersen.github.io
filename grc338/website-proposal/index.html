Introduction

J Jewelry Co.

J Jewelry Co. is local jewelry retailer in San Luis Obispo, CA. We have over twenty brands of jewelry and hundreds of styles to choose from so there is something for everyone. For the last ten years we have served the Central Coast with our amazing in-store shopping experience. Our team loves to help our customers find the perfect piece for every occation from birthdays, anniversary to proposals.

Target audience

The target audience of this site are people located in San Luis Obispo looking to buy jewelry from popular jewelry producers in an in-store environment. They want a one-stop shop to find the perfect piece of jewelry for themselves or as a gift for someone else. The audience does pre-shopping research and wants a website with information on products/services before going in-store.

Tasks: Shop for jewelry for themselves or others. Find their favorite jewelry brands. Research the jewelry they are considering buying. Goals: Find the perfect piece of jewelry for their occation. Be informed on the product their purchasing. Shop efficiently.

About Us

J Jewelry Co. is a small family owned jewelry store in the heart of the Central Coast. We are located in Downtown San Luis Obispo, CA. The Andersen family has been running J Jewelry Co. for the last ten years. We have grown our team over the years and currently have fifteen employees. J Jewelry Co. does in-store shopping only so we work directly with our customers.
